Mental modelMental model money has a direction

CI मदद क्यों लगता है, और lifecycle में कहां बैठता है।Why CI reads as help, and where it sits in the lifecycle.

▶
पैसे की दिशा —Money direction — Enforcement और Exit में पैसा CSP से दूर जाता है (bonus रुकना, freeze, कटौती, settlement)। Capability Intervention में यह CSP की ओर आता है — एक capped support credit। पूरा अनुभव मदद जैसा पढ़ना चाहिए, सज़ा जैसा नहीं।In Enforcement and Exit money moves away from the CSP (bonus pause, freeze, deduction, settlement). In Capability Intervention it moves toward them — a capped support credit. The whole experience must read as help, not penalty.
Lifecycle — Quality measures → Enforcement decides posture → Capability Intervention attempts recovery (you are here) → Exit ends participation. CI शुरू होता है Enforcement के CAPABILITY_RESET_TRIGGERED पर और तीन में से एक terminal outcome में खत्म होता है:CI starts on Enforcement's CAPABILITY_RESET_TRIGGERED and ends in exactly one of three terminal outcomes: recovery (→ normal) · exit-recommended (→ Exit B1) · voluntary exit mid-plan (→ Exit, a constitutional right). RESTRICTED ≠ SUSPENDED — RESTRICTED amber है (performance track, base कमाई जारी); लाल SUSPENDED hard-stop से जान-बूझकर अलग।RESTRICTED is amber (performance track, base pay continues); deliberately distinct from the red SUSPENDED hard stop.
